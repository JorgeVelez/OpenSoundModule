Getting Started

  1. Connect the OpenSoundModule to your rack. Make sure that the red stripe of the power cable is facing the -12v label on the module, and connect it so that the red stripe faces -12v on your modular bus board. Mount it with two screws.
  2. Switch the power on. After a moment the LED should flash then settle on green
  3. Enable WiFi on the device that you are connecting and log on to the network called OpenSoundModule as per the details below (Access Point Mode)
  4. You can now send OSC messages to the OSM on IP address 192.168.0.1, port 8000. You can receive messages from the OSM on port 9000
  5. Configure and control the OSM with Chrome by connecting to the OpenSoundModule network and navigating to URL http://192.168.0.1
